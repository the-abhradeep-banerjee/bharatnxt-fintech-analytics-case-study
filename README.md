## Project Headline

An **interactive Power BI analytics solution** designed to analyze **user lifecycle, transaction behavior, and revenue drivers** for a fintech platform, enabling **data-driven decisions to improve activation, retention, and monetization**.

Key findings include **~49% churn rate, ~66% transaction success rate, and limited (~10%) discount usage**, highlighting key areas for business improvement.

---

## Why This Project Matters

Fintech platforms often face a critical challenge — users sign up but fail to become **active, retained, and revenue-generating customers**.

This project addresses that challenge by transforming raw user and transaction data into a **decision-making system**, helping identify:
- Where users drop off  
- What drives engagement  
- How revenue is generated  
- Why users churn  

---

## Project Overview

BharatNXT is a fintech platform that enables SMEs to make business payments using credit cards, helping improve working capital flexibility.

This project builds a **comprehensive analytics dashboard** to analyze:
- User acquisition and activation funnel  
- Geographic and business segmentation  
- Churn behavior and retention patterns  
- Transaction trends and success rates  
- Pricing and discount impact on revenue  

The dashboard provides a **centralized analytical view** to support business strategy and operational decisions.

## Business Problem

BharatNXT aims to improve **user activation, retention, and revenue generation**.

However, the company lacks clear visibility into:
- Where users drop off in the funnel  
- Which acquisition channels bring high-quality users  
- Why activated users churn  
- How transaction behavior impacts revenue  

---

## Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this project is to build a **data-driven analytics system** that enables stakeholders to:

- Analyze the user lifecycle from registration to activation  
- Identify high-performing and low-quality acquisition channels  
- Understand churn behavior and retention gaps  
- Track transaction trends and platform performance  
- Evaluate pricing strategies and revenue impact  

---

## Key Analytical Questions

This dashboard was designed to answer the following:

- Where do users drop off in the funnel?
- Which channels bring high-quality vs low-quality users?
- Which regions show strong vs weak activation?
- Why do users churn despite activation?
- How are transaction volume and GTV trending over time?
- What is the transaction success rate across modes and months?
- How do discounts impact revenue and user behavior?

## Dashboard Features

This project consists of a **multi-page analytics dashboard** covering the full user lifecycle and transaction behavior.

---

## 1. User Funnel & Channel Analysis

### Purpose  
Analyzes user journey from registration to activation.

### Key Insights

- Significant drop-off observed between KYC completion and first transaction  
- Certain channels drive high registrations but low activation  

---

## 2. Geographic & Segment Analysis

### Purpose  
Examines performance across states, cities, and business types.

### Key Insights

- Some regions show strong registrations but weak activation  
- Business type segmentation highlights varying engagement levels  

---

## 3. Churn & Retention Analysis

### Purpose  
Identifies churn patterns and user behavior post-activation.

### Key Insights

- **~49% of activated users are churned**, indicating a significant post-activation drop-off
- Many churned users completed multiple transactions → retention issue, not activation  

---

## 4. Revenue & Transaction Trends

### Purpose  
Tracks transaction volume and GTV over time.

### Key Insights

- Revenue growth is driven more by volume than ticket size  
- Monthly trends show fluctuations indicating inconsistent growth  

---

## 5. Transaction Quality Analysis

### Purpose  
Evaluates transaction success rates and failures.

### Key Insights

- **~66% transaction success rate remains consistent across months**, indicating systemic platform-level issues rather than seasonal variation
- Indicates systemic platform-level issues rather than seasonal variation  

---

## 6. Pricing & Discount Analysis

### Purpose  
Analyzes impact of discounts on transactions and revenue.

### Key Insights

- **Only ~10% of transactions are discounted**, indicating selective usage
- Discounted transactions do not significantly increase transaction size, suggesting limited impact on user spending behavior
- Suggests discounts may not be effectively driving higher value transactions  

---

## Business Impact & Recommendations

This analysis enables BharatNXT to move toward **data-driven decision making**. Addressing these issues can significantly improve user retention, transaction success rates, and revenue efficiency

### Key Recommendations:

- Improve onboarding to reduce drop-off between KYC and first transaction  
- Focus on high-quality acquisition channels rather than volume  
- Introduce retention strategies (incentives, reminders) to reduce churn  
- Investigate and improve transaction reliability  
- Re-evaluate discount strategy for better revenue optimization
